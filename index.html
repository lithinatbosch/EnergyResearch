Smart Charging - intelligent electric vehicle charging: Bosch €246,000 + €15,858/year savings
  • Smart charging automatically schedules charging during low-cost electricity periods.
  • Reduces peak demand charges and improves charger utilization.
  • Extends battery life by avoiding unnecessary fast charging and high SoC dwell times.
Unlocking the Value of Public EV Chargers: A Data-Driven Case Study from Gothenburg, Sweden Reduce overall cost by ~74%
  • Real-world charging session data was used to optimize charging schedules.
  • Smart charging and V2G significantly increase charger utilization and grid flexibility.
  • Dynamic scheduling shifts charging to periods with lower electricity prices and higher renewable generation.
Commercial EV fleet smart charging for cost reduction and renewables integration: A case study in Germany Levelized Cost of Energy: €0.530 → €0.314/kW
  • Real-Time Pricing (RTP) enables charging when electricity prices are lowest.
  • Smart charging further reduced LCOE to €0.283/kWh by minimizing peak demand.
  • Integrating solar PV maximized renewable energy use while reducing operational costs.
Smart charging management for shared autonomous electric vehicle fleets: A Puget Sound case study 34-43% savings
  • Smart charging increased photovoltaic self-consumption to 99%.
  • Long-range EVs provided greater flexibility for optimized charging.
  • Coordinated charging reduced fleet energy costs while maintaining vehicle availability.
